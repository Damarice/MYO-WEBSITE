Our Impact

Since the foundation of the Maisha Yangu Organization in 2020, we have grown steadily, tripling both the number of children and the number of educational programs we offer and the positive impact of our community.

We accompany the children over a long period of time, build close relationships with them and thus have a strong positive influence on the individual and their life path. This is What makes our concept so unique and the positive impact so strong.
